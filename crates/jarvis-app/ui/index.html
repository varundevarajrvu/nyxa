How to use Nyxa

Wake me by clapping three times (or saying “Hey Jarvis” for now) — wait for the orb to brighten, then say what you want.

A “Hey Nyxa” voice wake is coming soon; for now, clapping is your quickest way in.

Open apps & websites

“open gmail”
“open spotify”
“open youtube and search for lo-fi beats”

Play music & videos

“play blinding lights on spotify”
“play a workout playlist on youtube”
“pause the music” · “next track” · “volume up”

Write an email

“write an email to john saying I’ll be late”

Opens a ready-to-review draft — it never sends on its own.

Close & tidy up asks first

“close spotify”
“close the youtube tab”
“delete report.pdf”

These ask before acting. For close/move Nyxa says “yes or no?” — just answer out loud. For delete it pops a window you must click.

Move it with your hands camera

Click Gesture (top-right) to turn on hand control. A small camera preview appears bottom-left.

  • One hand — move it around and the orb follows
  • Two hands — spread apart to zoom in, bring together to zoom out

All on-device — nothing leaves your laptop. Turn it back off to save battery.

Stopping Nyxa

Wake me and say “stop”, press Ctrl + Shift + Alt + K, or click Pause. It stays in the tray — reopen from the crimson orb icon.

Reading the orb

  • calm & slow — waiting for 3 claps or “Hey Jarvis”
  • bright with a ring — listening to your command
  • a crimson flare — doing it
  • dim & cold — paused
